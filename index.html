Noora
Naushad

Final-year PhD researcher in the NEST group, specialising in magnetic thin films and spintronic materials. Before Manchester, I earned an Integrated Master's in Physics from IISER TVM, India. My research focuses on fabricating and characterising L1₀ alloys, alongside building atomistic spin dynamics models of MnAl to understand its magnetic behaviour and potential as a permanent magnet.

Outside the lab, I oil paint, boulder with friends, and hike the Peak District looking for good sticks.
